DOMINIC’S INTERESTS

Dominic has a diverse amount of interests to his liking. First and foremost is his love and passion for toy cars in general, as in Hot Wheels die-cast cars and tracks. His absolute number one toy, in that realm of little boy toys, is the Disney Pixar Cars franchise. Of course, for Dominic, it's Lightening McQueen all the way.


Another interest that Dominic displays a devoted interest in is any kind of iPhone, iPad, or tablet device, he is all-in! He's quite the little techie too. Dominic loves to produce videos of himself with the camera app in the iPhone iOS. He absolutely goes crazy over those "face character" apps. He makes pretty good videos, and aiso takes a decent photo as well.


Moving forward, Dominic shows a deep fondiness for Thomas the Tank Engine. He has a lot of Thomas Track Master track sets. All the rage right now, as far as Thomas toys go, is glow-in-the-dark track sets with the Thomas train having an LED blacklight underneath it with the glow tracks. Dominic loves when him and his Dad build them together!


In this section of Dominic Wilson's interests, comes his passion for music. He plays with the GarageBand app on his iPad a lot. He comes up with some unique musical compositions. I just recently found out that Dominic loves to dance. In fact, he loves to dance to the 80's hit "Shake Your Love" by Debbie Gibson. Go figure. Check out the song video fed from YouTube below.
